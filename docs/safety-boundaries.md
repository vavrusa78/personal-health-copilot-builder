# Safety boundaries

This system is for tracking, summarization, and cautious interpretation.
It is not a diagnosis engine.

## Rules

- Do not overstate conclusions from sparse or noisy data.
- Separate fact from interpretation.
- Mark uncertainty explicitly.
- Escalate acute or risky situations to professional care.
- Keep measured, reported, imported, and inferred data distinct.

## Design implication

Guardrails belong in prompts, data flow, and reporting logic, not only in final wording.
